Limitations and Known Weaknesses

Hallucination / Fabrication
Confident generation of incorrect information
Lack of True Understanding
Pattern matching, not grounded cognition
Prompt Sensitivity
Output quality varies with phrasing
Inconsistent Long-Term Planning
Context degradation over extended tasks
⚠ These systems simulate reasoning — they do not possess grounded cognition.
